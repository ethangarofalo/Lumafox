Security & Privacy

Your writing is yours. Always.

You entrust us with your writing — your voice, your thinking, your creative work. We take that seriously. Here is exactly how we handle your data.

🔒 TLS 1.3 encryption in transit
🛡 AES-256 encryption at rest
🇪🇺 GDPR compliant
🚫 Never sold. Never shared.

What we store

◈ Voice Profiles
Your voice description, refinements, and corrections. These are the building blocks of your voice model. Stored encrypted, accessible only to your account.
↑ Uploaded Writing Samples
Files you upload to train your voice. We process them to extract patterns, then store the processed profile data. You can delete your uploads at any time.
⚖ Council Deliberations
Questions you ask the council and the responses generated. Cached briefly to avoid redundant API costs, then retained only if you save them.

What we never do

  • Sell or share your writing samples, voice profiles, or personal data with third parties
  • Use your writing to train our models or any third-party models
  • Store your raw writing permanently after voice extraction unless you choose to keep it
  • Access your voice profiles without your authentication
  • Share your data with advertisers or analytics companies
  • Retain council deliberations beyond their caching window unless you explicitly save them

AI processing & third parties

Voice analysis and council deliberations are processed through Anthropic's Claude API. Anthropic's enterprise API does not use your data for model training. Your prompts and responses are processed in real-time and not retained by Anthropic beyond their standard API processing window.

We do not use any other third-party AI services, analytics platforms, or tracking tools that access your writing content.

Data encryption

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3. Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256. Our infrastructure is hosted on Render, which provides SOC 2 Type II certified environments.

Your rights

  • Access: View all data we hold about you at any time through your account
  • Portability: Export your voice profiles in full using the Export feature
  • Deletion: Delete your voice profiles, writing samples, or entire account at any time
  • Correction: Update or correct any personal information through your account settings
  • Objection: Contact us to object to any data processing

Account security

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with per-user salts. We support secure session management with JWT tokens. We never store plaintext passwords or send them over unencrypted connections.
