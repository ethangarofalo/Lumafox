How It Works
01
Teach it your principles
Tell it how you write in plain language. Short sentences. No adverbs. Never start with a subordinate clause. Whatever is true for you.
02
Upload your past work
Drop in essays, articles, emails, or scripts. The voice learns your patterns directly from what you've already written.
03
Generate and correct
Write something. When it's off, correct it on the spot — the correction is saved as a refinement and the voice retries immediately.
04
Export it anywhere
Copy the distilled voice prompt and paste it into any AI tool you already use — ChatGPT, Claude, Notion AI, wherever you work.

Honest by design

Lumafox is not for everyone.
That's the point.

Lumafox is for you if —
◆ You have a distinct way of thinking and want AI to amplify it, not erase it
◆ You make decisions you actually care about and want rigorous perspective before acting
◆ You're a writer, thinker, or professional whose voice is a competitive advantage
◆ You want to get sharper over time — not just faster
◆ You believe that disagreement, done well, leads somewhere truer than consensus
This isn't for you if —
◇ You need generic content at scale and don't care whose voice it is
◇ You want an AI that tells you what you want to hear
◇ You're looking for a shortcut around thinking, not a tool that deepens it
◇ You have no interest in your own writing improving — just in producing more of it
◇ You want quick answers, not examined ones
