The first AI writing tool
built around you,
not around prompts.

Every general AI tool writes fluently. None of them write like you. Voice is different. It builds a precise, trainable model of your writing — your rhythm, your rules, your instincts — and gets sharper every time you use it.

The model isn't a persona or a template. It's a living record of how you think and communicate, built entirely from what you teach it and what you correct it on. The more you use it, the more it sounds like you and no one else.

How It Works
01
Teach it your principles
Tell it how you write in plain language. Short sentences. No adverbs. Never start with a subordinate clause. Whatever is true for you.
02
Upload your past work
Drop in essays, articles, emails, or scripts. The voice learns your patterns directly from what you've already written.
03
Generate and correct
Write something. When it's off, correct it on the spot — the correction is saved as a refinement and the voice retries immediately.
04
Export it anywhere
Copy the distilled voice prompt and paste it into any AI tool you already use — ChatGPT, Claude, Notion AI, wherever you work.
Why It's Different
vs. General AI
It knows it's wrong when it doesn't sound like you
ChatGPT doesn't know your style exists. Voice is built on the premise that your writing has a specific logic — and that logic can be taught, tested, and exported.
vs. Style guides
It improves through use, not just documentation
A style guide sits still. Voice accumulates refinements every time you correct it — it gets more precise the longer you use it.
vs. Fine-tuned models
No engineering required
No datasets, no training runs, no API keys to configure. Teach it in plain English, correct it in plain English, use it immediately.
